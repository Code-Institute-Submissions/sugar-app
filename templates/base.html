About

A healthy diet means limiting our intake of added sugar. The guidelines are no more than 6 teaspoons (24g) of added sugar per day for women and 9 teaspoons (36g) for men. The Sugar Catalog is your reference to identify high sugar foods and their low sugar alternatives. If your favourite food is missing from the catalog, please consider adding it. Test your knowledge of added sugar by taking our quick survey.
